About Us

When Barboth arrived on The Blue Planet in the summer of 1997, no one knew how to make an accessible website. This was really too bad, because everyone needs an accessible website.

In those days, a small business was lucky to have a website at all. And if they did have a website, it probably had a horrible <marquee> scrolling across the page. Worst of all, hardly any of them were blue.

Fortunately, Barboth's craft landed in upstate New York and brought the joys of accessible web design to the most important web services at the time. The rest is history.
